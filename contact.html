Frequently Asked Questions

How do I list an item?

Simply create an account, take photos of your item, add a description and price, and your listing will be live within minutes.

Is it safe to buy from strangers?

We recommend meeting in public places for exchanges and trusting your instincts. We also have user ratings and reviews to help you make informed decisions.

What fees do you charge?

Our platform is free to use for basic listings. We may introduce premium features in the future, but our core mission remains accessible to everyone.

How do I report a problem?

Use the contact form above or email us directly. We take all reports seriously and aim to respond within 24 hours.
