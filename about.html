About Used

Connecting people with pre-loved items for a sustainable future

Our Mission

At Used, we believe that every item deserves a second chance. Our platform connects people who have items they no longer need with those who are looking for exactly those things.

We're passionate about creating a more sustainable world by extending the lifecycle of products and reducing waste. Every transaction on our platform represents a small victory for the environment and a step towards a circular economy.

Why It Matters

The average household contains thousands of items, many of which are used infrequently or not at all. Meanwhile, millions of people are looking for affordable alternatives to buying new. We bridge this gap by making it easy to find and share used items.

Our Story

Used was born from a simple observation: there's an abundance of perfectly good items sitting unused in homes, while people elsewhere are searching for those exact things. We started with the goal of creating a simple, user-friendly platform that would make it easy for people to connect and share.

What began as a small project has grown into a community of thousands of people who are passionate about sustainable living and giving items a second life. Every day, our users help reduce waste, save money, and discover unique treasures.

We're continuously working to improve the platform and make it easier for people to participate in the circular economy. Our vision is a world where nothing useful goes to waste, and everyone has access to the items they need.
